The Right to Repair Movement




What is Right to Repair?

Right to repair is a consumer rights movement that aims to liberate and improve the current consumer electronics market. It aims for sustainable electronics as methods and materials manufacturing them and also easy user repairability.

Why should you join this movement

We believe that consumers such as you drive the current consumer electronics market and innovation forward. We also believe that you can change it, mold it as our vision for a sustainable, cleaner, better world.
